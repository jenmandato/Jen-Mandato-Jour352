10.  Why do we use media queries?

We use media queries to tailor style sheets to different types of devices (computers, mobile phones and tablets, etc.). They can be used to evaluate things like dimensions of the viewport and device, device orientation and resolution.

11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

The main difference between a serif and sans-serif font is that serif fonts have a tail/stroke on them, whereas sans-serif fonts do not. This can make serif fonts seem very elegant and sans-serif fonts more modern or minimal.
